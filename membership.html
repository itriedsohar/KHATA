Membership



Karamoja Hoteliers and Tours Association (KHATA) is an association of indigenous
hotel and tour company owners in the Karamoja region, Uganda, numbering about 50
facilities in Karamoja, formed in 2019 and incorporated in 2023. Some of the association members are also members of Uganda Hotelier Association (UHOA) and Association of
Uganda Tours and Operators (AUTO), Uganda Tourism Board (UTB) and Uganda Wildlife Authority (UWA).



Rights of members

Full members


  • Attend meetings of the general assembly

  • Pay annual subsription fee as set out in the schedule of this constitution

  • Pay monthly savings as set out in the schedule of this constitution

  • Respect any other Duties that may be prescribed by the general assembly from time to time

  • Vote and stand for electable positons In the association

Duties of members


  • All memebers must abide by the constitution of polices and regulations of the association

  • Members shall participate in the activities of the association

  • Members shall have the right for appeal before the general assembly

  • A memeber shall have the right to nomintae or be nominate to the positions of the association

  • All members shall enjoy the facilities and services offered by the association subject to the policies and regulations of the association

  • Access the information relating to the associations operations, upon request of the same from the executive committee

Financial Targets

  • Year 1 Goal:
    • Raise UGX 100 million through member contributions.

  • Long-Term Goal: Build KHATA RDF to UGX 50 billion through partnerships with the government, donors, and financial institutions.
