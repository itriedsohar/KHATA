Welcome to


Karimojong Hoteliers and Tourism Association (KHATA)


KHATA’s mission is to empower indigenous businesses to compete effectively in the growing tourism industry by standardizing services, expanding infrastructure, and promoting Karamoja as a premier tourist destination.


Visit Karamoja


Karamoja offers a unique experience with its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and warm-hearted communities. A visit to this beautiful region allows you to explore traditional lifestyles, breathtaking wildlife, and vibrant local markets. It’s a destination for adventure and cultural immersion in Uganda.

(KHATA) sister hotels


Our member hotels offer a perfect blend of comfort and tradition, providing an unforgettable stay. Immerse yourself in the region’s rich cultural heritage, explore stunning landscapes, and enjoy personalized hospitality that will make your visit truly special. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Karamoja’s hoteliers and tourism partners are ready to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

Message from the chairperson



The Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association extends a warm greeting, highlighting the region's unique cultural heritage and stunning landscapes. The association celebrates the role of local hotels, lodges, and guesthouses in providing quality accommodation and supporting sustainable livelihoods. Looking to the future, the association is committed to promoting responsible tourism, advocating for members' welfare, and fostering a tourism ecosystem that benefits both visitors and local communities. The message expresses gratitude for continued support and emphasizes the importance of working together for a brighter future for Karamoja's tourism sector.

Hon. Lokeris Samson
Chairman, Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association
