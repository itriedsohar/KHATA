Administration

Message from the chairperson


The Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association extends a warm greeting, highlighting the region's unique cultural heritage and stunning landscapes. The association celebrates the role of local hotels, lodges, and guesthouses in providing quality accommodation and supporting sustainable livelihoods. Looking to the future, the association is committed to promoting responsible tourism, advocating for members' welfare, and fostering a tourism ecosystem that benefits both visitors and local communities. The message expresses gratitude for continued support and emphasizes the importance of working together for a brighter future for Karamoja's tourism sector.

Hon. Lokeris Samson
Chairman, Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association

Message from the General Secretary


The General Secretary of the Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association expresses enthusiasm and responsibility in serving the association. He emphasizes the region's beauty, cultural heritage, and potential as a tourism hub. The association has played a vital role in advocating for the interests of hotel owners, offering training and development opportunities, and ensuring quality service. Looking ahead, he encourages collaboration, responsible tourism practices, and sustainable growth to enhance Karamoja's reputation as a top tourist destination. He thanks members for their support and urges continued dedication to the cause.

Kind regards,

Mr.Limlim Phillip lomma
General Secretary, Karamoja Hotel Owners and Tourism Association
