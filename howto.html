Eligibility of membership

NOTE


  • Membership is by business entity.

  • There shall be two types of membership,i.e, Founder Members and Members admitted in accordance with KHATA constitution.

    • Business is solely or substantially (majority) owned by a Karamojong.

    • Operates a bussiness in ·the hospitality and or tourism sector.

    • In case of a founder member, had been admitted as of 30th October 2023.

      • For others;

      • has been vetted over a period of over six months by Exetcutive Committee

      • Has been approved by the General Assembly

      • Pays a one off membership fee as specified in the schedule to KHATA constitu1iion

      • Pays an annual subscription fee set out in schedule to KHATA constitutiton

      • Makes a monthly saving set out in schedule of KHATA constitution. Such can be paid monthly or in lump sum

procedure for admission of members


An aspiring Member shall;

  • apply to the General Secretary

  • the General Secretary upon receiption of such application shall present it to the Executive Committee which will consider and make recommendations to the general assembly,who at a meeting, will admit or reject the application
